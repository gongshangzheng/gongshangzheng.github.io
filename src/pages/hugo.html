equal

In Hugo, there exist 3 type of so-called equal, they are:
- :=: to equal the variable to a value
- =: to equal the variable to another variable
- eq

wiki.en CNN

``html
{/{if eq $display_text }}
{/{display_text := $text}}
{/{- end -}}
here is the text: {$text}

here is the displayed text:{$display_text}

Is displayed text empty? :{/{eq $display_text }}

` `output

here is the text: CNN
here is the displayed text:
Is displayed text empty? :true
CNN

`
